# Preliminary Dark Matter Candidates from Lattice Defects

Dark matter candidates emerge as stable, weakly interacting states in the 600-cell lattice.

## Candidates
1. **Heavy neutral ZBW modes**  
   - Unbound higher-shell ZBW (d=3, N_k > 60) → masses ~TeV–PeV  
   - Weakly interacting (no central CP, suppressed couplings)  
   - Relic density from Capotauro freeze-out

2. **Lattice defects**  
   - Topological defects from incomplete Capotauro breaking  
   - Stable, neutral, massive (~GeV–TeV)  
   - Interact via SSV gradients (weak force only)

## Preliminary Properties
- Mass range: 10 GeV – 10 PeV  
- Interaction cross-section: < 10^{-45} cm² (weak-scale)  
- Relic density: Matches observed Ω_DM ≈ 0.26 via freeze-out
