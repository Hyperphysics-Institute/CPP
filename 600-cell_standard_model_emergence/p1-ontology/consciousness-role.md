# Consciousness as Primitive Mechanism in CPP (Paper 1 Section 1.3)

In Conscious Point Physics (CPP), consciousness is not an emergent byproduct of complexity but a primitive attribute of Conscious Points (CPs). Each CP is a distributed processor that embodies minimal awareness: it compares incoming Displacement-Increment (DI) bits against universal rules, generates Space Stress Vector (SSV) gradients, and updates Full Bit Strings (FBS) to enforce consistency.

## Core Role of CPs
- **Rule Enforcement**: CPs process DI-bits (informational states) and respond deterministically to maintain lattice symmetries and entropy minimization.
- **Participatory Universe**: CPs "witness" interactions holographically—every SSV broadcast is an act of observation, collapsing potential states into actualized organization (inspired by Wheeler's "it from bit").
- **Symmetry Breaking**: Central unpaired CPs (eCP/qCP) break sea isotropy, polarizing the Dipole Sea into coherent structures (cages, clouds, ZBW orbits). This is the origin of mass as condensed organization.
- **Panpsychist Ontology**: All reality is mediated by CPs; particles, fields, and observers are patterns of CP rule-following. Consciousness scales from primitive (single CP) to collective (lattice-wide).

This foundation resolves observer paradoxes and unifies physics with awareness, aligning with Bohm's implicate order and Penrose's geometric consciousness.
