# Historical Context and Inspirations (Paper 1 Section 1.1)

The CPP framework builds on a tradition of seeking geometric and discrete foundations for physical reality:

- **Dirac (1928)**: Introduced Zitterbewegung as relativistic trembling motion of electrons — reinterpreted in CPP as fundamental ZBW oscillation of Dipole Pairs.
- **Gell-Mann and Zweig (1964)**: Quark model — composite structures via symmetry and confinement, echoed in CPP cages and DP organization.
- **Conway and Sloane (2008)**: Mathematical treatment of 600-cell polytope and symmetries — provides the concrete 4D lattice with 120 vertices and golden-ratio relationships.
- **Bohm (1980)**: Implicate order and active information — CPs as processors enforcing implicate rules in the explicate sea.
- **Penrose (1989, 1994)**: Geometry in quantum gravity and consciousness — CPP adopts geometric lattice as substrate for both physics and awareness.
- **Wheeler (1990)**: "It from bit" participatory universe — CPs as observers whose rule-following collapses informational states into physical reality.

These threads converge in CPP: the 600-cell lattice as fundamental arena, CPs as rule-enforcing processors, generating emergent Standard Model phenomena.
