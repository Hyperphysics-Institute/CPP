# Current Experimental Neutrino Mixing Angles

From NuFIT 5.3 / 2025–2026 global fit (best-fit values, approximate):

- θ₁₂ ≈ 33.44° (sin²θ₁₂ ≈ 0.304 ± 0.012)
- θ₂₃ ≈ 49.0° (sin²θ₂₃ ≈ 0.570 ± 0.024, octant ambiguity)
- θ₁₃ ≈ 8.57° (sin²θ₁₃ ≈ 0.0220 ± 0.0006)
- δ_CP ≈ 195° ± 40° (preferred, could be ~ -165° in some fits)

Normal ordering is strongly preferred; inverted ordering disfavored.
