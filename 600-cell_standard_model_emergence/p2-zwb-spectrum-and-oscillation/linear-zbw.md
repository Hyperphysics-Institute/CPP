# Linear ZBW Extras (d=1): Mass Boost in Down-Type Quarks

Linear ZBW occurs in down-type quarks as extra qDP/hDP pairs oscillating back-and-forth along SSV gradient lines (d=1, σ ≈ 8.33 × 10^{-3}).

## Characteristics
- Only down-type (−qCP central) due to Capotauro chiral-polarity bias (Appendix H).
- Adds second charge screening layer (Appendix G).
- Energy: E_DP = ½ m (c / r_k)² · σ per extra — partial suppression from unbound dimension.

## Role
Contributes ~20-40% extra mass to down-type quarks relative to up-type (explains m_d > m_u). No spin contribution (linear, no net angular momentum).
