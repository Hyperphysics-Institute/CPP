# Bound Orbital ZBW (d=0): Spin and Magnetic Moment

Bound orbital ZBW occurs in fully bound modes (d=0, σ=1) where the dipole pair orbits a central unpaired CP within a tight lattice subgroup (e.g., tetrahedral cage).

## Characteristics
- **Leptons**: Orbital eDP-dominant (~68.5% eDP, 13% qDP, 18.5% hDP for muon-like; lower mixing for electron). Generates spin-1/2 and magnetic moment via orbital current.
- **Quarks**: Orbital qDP-dominant (~74% qDP for up-type; similar for down but with linear extras).
- **Energy Contribution**: E_ZBW = ½ m (c / √N_k)² — scales with cage size (N_k).
- **No Net Field from Cloud**: Orbital is single DP; cloud linear ZBW averages spherically (no net magnetism).

## Role
Primary source of intrinsic spin and g-factor (g=2 from relativistic orbital motion, per Dirac equation). Mass contribution: ~20% for electron (E_eDP = 0.102 MeV).
