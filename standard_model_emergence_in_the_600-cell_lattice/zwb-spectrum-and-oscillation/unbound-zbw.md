# Unbound Orbital ZBW (d=3): Neutrino Masses

Unbound orbital ZBW is self-contained oscillation of minimal DP structures with no central CP (d=3, σ ≈ 5.8 × 10^{-7}).

## Characteristics
- **ν_e**: Single orbital eDP
- **ν_μ**: Single orbital qDP
- **ν_τ**: Tetrahedral hDP cluster (4 interconnected DPs)
- Energy: E_spin = ½ m v² · σ — maximal suppression from 3 unbound dimensions.

## Role
Produces tiny masses (~10^{-9} to 10^{-8} MeV) with normal ordering hierarchy. Sum ~0.017 eV (below cosmological bounds).
