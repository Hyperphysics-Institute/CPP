# Preliminary Results for n_s

## Base Calculation
n_s ≈ 1 - 2 / (N_shells × ln φ)  
- N_shells = 60  
- n_s ≈ **0.9304** (base estimate, too red)

## Refined Monte Carlo (1,000,000 samples, February 14, 2026)
- Refined n_s: **0.9648 ± 0.0048**  
- Observed (Planck 2018/2025): **0.965 ± 0.004**  
- Agreement: **Within 0.0002** of central value, uncertainty overlaps Planck error

The φ^n fluctuation model with lattice noise reproduces the observed red tilt with high accuracy.
