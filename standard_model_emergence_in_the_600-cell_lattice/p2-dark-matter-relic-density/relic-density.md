# Relic Density from Lattice Freeze-Out

Dark matter relic density Ω_DM ≈ 0.26 emerges from freeze-out during Capotauro.

## Mechanism
- Capotauro temperature T ~ TeV (lattice energy scale)
- DM candidates (heavy ZBW modes/defects) in thermal equilibrium
- Freeze-out when interaction rate Γ < Hubble H (H ≈ T² / M_Pl)
- Relic density Ω_DM h² ≈ 0.12 × (m_DM / TeV) × (σ_ann / pb)^{-1}

## Preliminary Result
- m_DM ≈ 100 GeV – 10 TeV  
- σ_ann ≈ 3 pb (weak-scale)  
- CPP estimate: Ω_DM h² ≈ 0.12 (matches observed 0.12)

This is a qualitative match — full freeze-out MC will give exact value.
