# Derivation of Gravitational Constant G

G emerges from the global curvature of the 600-cell lattice and the Planck-scale mass density induced by CP interactions.

## Mechanism
- Lattice curvature κ is computed from the full 120-vertex mode structure (global curvature radius)
- Planck-scale mass density ρ_Pl ≈ E_P / (Planck volume)
- Effective G ≈ κ / ρ_Pl (dimensional analysis in lattice units)
