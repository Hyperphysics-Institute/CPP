# Charge Screening and Asymmetries in Conscious Point Physics (CPP)

This directory documents the mechanisms behind fractional quark charges (+2/3 for up-type, -1/3 for down-type), the up/down quark asymmetry, and related asymmetries, all emerging from Space Stress Vector (SSV) screening in orbital and linear Zitterbewegung (ZBW) combined with the Capotauro chiral-polarity bias in the 600-cell lattice.

These are not ad hoc parameters but derive from lattice invariants (golden ratio φ ≈ 1.618, vertex count 120) and the chiral nucleation event Capotauro (~120 million years post-Big Bang).

Cross-references: Paper 2 Appendices G (charge reduction), H (up/down asymmetry), L (golden ratio approximations), Section 5 (ZBW refinements).

## Key Mechanisms Summary
- **Fractional Charge Reduction**: SSV compression in orbital ZBW yields ~1/3 screening via 1/φ² ≈ 0.382, refined to exact 1/3 with ε ≈ -0.145 from multi-layer averaging.
- **Up/Down Asymmetry**: Capotauro bias favors linear ZBW extras on down-type (−qCP central), enabling second 1/3 screening → -1/3 effective charge.
- **Antimatter Mirror**: Lattice duals invert bias in anti-sea domains, preserving charge symmetry under CPT.
- **Broader Asymmetries**: Capotauro crystallizes 8 major asymmetries (baryon, chirality, CP violation, arrow of time, up/down, generations, CMB hemisphere, black hole spin).
