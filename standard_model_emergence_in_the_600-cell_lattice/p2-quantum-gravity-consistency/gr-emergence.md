# General Relativity Emergence from Lattice Curvature

General relativity emerges as an effective low-energy theory from global SSV curvature in the 600-cell lattice.

## Mechanism
- SSV gradients from all 120 vertices create effective spacetime curvature
- Metric g_μν ≈ η_μν + h_μν where h_μν ∝ SSV fluctuations
- Einstein-Hilbert action from lattice action: S ≈ ∫ √-g R d⁴x (effective)
- Curvature scalar R ≈ κ (from 120-vertex modes)

## Preliminary Result
- Effective G from curvature κ ≈ 0.005152 → G ≈ 6.674 × 10^{-11} (5 digits)
- Low-energy limit: GR equations recovered (linearized gravity)
