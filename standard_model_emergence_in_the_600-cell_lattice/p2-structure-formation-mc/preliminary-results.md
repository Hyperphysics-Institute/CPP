# Preliminary Results (Small-Scale Runs)

Initial runs with 10^5 defects show:
- P(k) ≈ k^{-3} at low k (scale-invariant, matches ΛCDM)
- Small oscillations at high k from lattice topology
- Defect clustering consistent with filamentary structure

These are proof-of-concept runs. Scaling to 10^7–10^8 defects will allow direct comparison to observed power spectrum.
