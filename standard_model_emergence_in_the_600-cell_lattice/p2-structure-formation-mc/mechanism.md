# Mechanism: Structure Formation from Lattice Defects

Large-scale structure is seeded by topological defects from incomplete Capotauro symmetry breaking.

## Mechanism
- During Capotauro, some lattice bonds fail to align → stable defects
- Defects have mass ~GeV–TeV and interact weakly via residual SSV gradients
- Defects act as primordial density seeds → gravitational collapse → galaxies and filaments
- Power spectrum P(k) from defect correlation function (lattice topology imprints oscillations at high k)

## Predictions
- Low-k: nearly scale-invariant (matches ΛCDM)
- High-k: oscillations and cutoff from lattice discreteness (testable with future surveys)
