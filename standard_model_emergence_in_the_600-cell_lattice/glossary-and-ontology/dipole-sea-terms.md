# Dipole Sea and Organization Terms

- **Dipole Sea**  
  Medium filling space: random oscillating DPs (eDP, qDP, hDP-A, hDP-B). Maximizes entropy → uniform 25% baseline.

- **Organizational Energy**  
  Energy cost of imposing order on the sea. Manifests as rest mass (E = mc²). More organization → higher mass.

- **Polarization**  
  Radial bias of DP orientations by central CP. Static average + dynamic ZBW maintenance.

- **Entropic Pressure**  
  Sea's drive to randomize → outward diffusion countering gradients.
