# CMB μ-Distortions from Sea Fluctuations

CMB μ-distortions arise from Dipole Sea fluctuations.

## Mechanism
- Sea ZBW oscillations create small temperature perturbations
- Amplitude ~10^{-8} at high ℓ (ℓ ≳ 3000)
- Prediction from Paper 2 Section 7.2

## Status
- Consistent with current upper bounds (~10^{-7})
- Next: compute power spectrum from lattice noise
