# GW Spectrum Rollover

Gravitational wave spectrum rolls off above lattice cutoff.

## Mechanism
- Lattice granularity sets minimum wavelength ~ l_Pl
- Cutoff frequency > 10^{10} Hz (Planck scale)

## Prediction
- No GWs above ~10^{10} Hz — testable with future detectors
