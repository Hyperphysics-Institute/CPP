# Zitterbewegung (ZBW) and Spin Terms

- **Zitterbewegung (ZBW)**  
  Fundamental oscillation of DPs at f ≈ 1/(2 t_Pl). Attraction-repulsion cycle driven by SSV gradient flips.

- **Orbital ZBW DP**  
  Circular motion around central CP (or self-orbiting). Produces spin-1/2 and magnetic moment.

- **Linear ZBW DP**  
  Back-and-forth oscillation along gradients. Occurs in down-type quark extras; adds mass and screening.

- **Unbound Orbital ZBW**  
  Self-contained orbital motion (neutrinos). Maximal suppression (d=3).

- **SSV Gradient Flip**  
  Inversion of net gradient when +limb nears central CP → cycle reset.
