# Miscellaneous Terms

- **VEV (Vacuum Expectation Value)**  
  Chiral condensate energy scale: ⟨ϕ⟩ = k · E_P / N_lattice⁴ · ϕ_k. Sets mass scale.

- **Iterative Solve**  
  Algorithm to compute m by adding refinements iteratively until convergence.

- **DI-bits**  
  Informational currency for rule enforcement by CPs.

- **FBS (Full Bit String)**  
  Broadcast of CP state to all GPs within Planck Sphere Radius.
