# Geometric and Lattice Terms

- **600-cell lattice**  
  Perfect 4D polytope with 120 vertices, 600 tetrahedral 4-cells, icosahedral symmetry. Fundamental grid of reality in CPP; CPs occupy vertices.

- **Capotauro**  
  Chiral nucleation event (~120 million years post-Big Bang) when lattice crystallizes from Dipole Sea. Activates handedness and polarity bias, producing up/down asymmetry and other asymmetries.

- **SSV (Space Stress Vector)**  
  Local curvature/tension field in Dipole Sea from CPs. Inverse-square falloff along hyperedges. Drives attraction/repulsion and ZBW flips.

- **Golden ratio (φ ≈ 1.618)**  
  Intrinsic to 600-cell edges, dihedral angles, shell spacings. Generates charge screening (1/φ² ≈ 1/3), generational scaling, and α_em approximation.

- **N_k**  
  Cage occupancy/vertex count: 1 (minimal), 4 (tetra), 12 (icosa), 20 (dodeca), 60 (fullerene-like). Scales mass via bonding and r_cloud.
