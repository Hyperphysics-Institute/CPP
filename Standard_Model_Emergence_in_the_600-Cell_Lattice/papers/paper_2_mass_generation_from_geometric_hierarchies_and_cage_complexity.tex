\begin{abstract}
This paper reconceptualizes mass generation in Conscious Point Physics (CPP) as arising from symmetry breaking during the Capotauro chiral nucleation event, creating a vacuum expectation value (VEV) that couples to lattice cages via Yukawa-like terms derived from 600-cell geometry. The VEV is derived from Planck scales with holographic suppression from lattice entropy. We provide complete step-by-step derivations for all Standard Model leptons (e, μ, τ, neutrinos), quarks (u,d,s,c,b,t), and massive bosons (W, Z, Higgs), predicting masses ab initio and comparing to observed values. Additional refinements include ZBW energy from eDP/hDP oscillations, extra ZBW hDP for down-top quarks, ZBW eDP for fermion spin, and specific neutrino compositions (eDP for electron neutrino, qDP for muon neutrino, A and B hDP spinning tetrahedron for tau neutrino). The framework resolves scaling issues, provides geometric origins for hierarchies, and achieves reasonable agreement as a first approximation without parameter tuning.
\end{abstract}
