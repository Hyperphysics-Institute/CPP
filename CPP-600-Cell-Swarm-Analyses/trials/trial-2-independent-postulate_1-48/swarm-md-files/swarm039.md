## Left-Right Asymmetry Analysis

### Physical Description
Laboratory magnetic reconnection experiments exhibit systematic preferences in current sheet twist handedness that correlate with initial magnetic field configuration and plasma beta parameters. These asymmetries manifest as reproducible chirality in the helical structure of current sheets during reconnection events, preferred sense of magnetic field line twisting, and correlations between twist handedness and reconnection rate that exceed expectations from symmetric magnetohydrodynamic models, potentially indicating kinetic physics effects, Hall term influences, or systematic experimental asymmetries in magnetic field geometry.
