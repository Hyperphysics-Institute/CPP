## Left-Right Asymmetry Analysis

### Study Information

- **Scale**: Planetary Geology Physics (tidal tectonics)

### Physical Description
Europa's ice shell exhibits systematic asymmetries in tectonic stress patterns that manifest as preferred orientations in lineae systems, asymmetric ridge and trough morphologies, and correlations between surface feature geometry and orbital position relative to Jupiter. These asymmetries exceed expectations from simple tidal stress models and show hemisphere-to-hemisphere variations in fracture density and orientation that correlate with the underlying ocean circulation patterns, potentially indicating complex ice shell-ocean interactions, non-synchronous rotation effects, or subsurface thermal variations.

- **Precision**: Feature orientation uncertainties ~5-15 degrees depending on image resolution
