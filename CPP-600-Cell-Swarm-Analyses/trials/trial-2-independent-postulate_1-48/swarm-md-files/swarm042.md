# Swarm042: Ocean Current Vorticity Asymmetries
## Left-Right Asymmetry Analysis

### Study Information
- **Phenomenon**: Systematic asymmetries in ocean mesoscale eddy vorticity distributions between cyclonic and anticyclonic circulation
- **Scale**: Oceanographic Physics (geostrophic turbulence)
- **Experimental Context**: Satellite altimetry and autonomous float observations
- **Time Period**: 2016-2024 measurements
- **Selection Date**: January 24, 2026
- **Selected By**: Claude (Swarm)

### Physical Description
Ocean mesoscale eddies exhibit systematic asymmetries in vorticity intensity and structure between cyclonic (negative vorticity) and anticyclonic (positive vorticity) circulation patterns that vary with latitude, stratification, and background current shear. These asymmetries manifest as differences in eddy amplitude, lifetime, and propagation characteristics that correlate with planetary vorticity and nonlinear dynamics, showing reproducible deviations from symmetric geostrophic turbulence predictions, potentially indicating beta-plane effects, stratification influences, or systematic forcing asymmetries in ocean circulation.

### Experimental Setup
- **System**: Mesoscale ocean eddies in subtropical and subpolar ocean basins
- **Instruments**: TOPEX/Poseidon/Jason satellite altimetry, Argo profiling float network

- **Observable**: Ocean eddy vorticity asymmetry A_vorticity from circulation intensity distributions
- **Precision**: Sea surface height uncertainties ~2-5 cm, current velocity ~5-10 cm/s
- **Controls**: Altimeter drift corrections, geoid model uncertainties, seasonal variability

### Expected Asymmetry Measurement
**Primary Observable**: Ocean mesoscale vorticity asymmetry A_vorticity
- **Definition**: A_vorticity = (|ω_cyclonic| - |ω_anticyclonic|)/(|ω_cyclonic| + |ω_anticyclonic|) for eddy vorticity magnitudes
- **Statistical Power**: Typically 4-6σ significance with global eddy tracking datasets
- **Direction**: Empirical measurement compared to symmetric geostrophic turbulence predictions
- **Scale**: ~10⁻² to 10⁻¹ relative vorticity asymmetry depending on latitude and stratification
