### Physical Description
Dark matter direct detection experiments observe systematic asymmetries in nuclear recoil directions that correlate with Earth's motion through the dark matter halo. These asymmetries manifest as preferred recoil directions relative to the expected dark matter wind direction, potentially indicating either genuine dark matter interactions with directional sensitivity or previously unrecognized backgrounds with inherent directional preferences.

- **Method**: Nuclear recoil direction reconstruction and seasonal modulation analysis
- **Observable**: Recoil direction asymmetry A_recoil
- **Precision**: Energy thresholds ~1-10 keV with direction sensitivity where available
